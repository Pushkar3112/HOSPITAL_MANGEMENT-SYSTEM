GENERAL GUIDELINES FOR ALL PATIENTS
=========================================================================
1. Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time
2. Carry a valid photo ID (Aadhaar card, Passport, or Driver's License)
3. Carry your health insurance card if applicable
4. Bring all previous medical records, test reports, and prescriptions
5. Inform the receptionist about any allergies, especially to medications
6. For children under 18: a parent or legal guardian must be present
7. If you need to cancel, please call at least 2 hours in advance
8. In case of emergency, go directly to the Emergency Department (ground floor)
9. Telemedicine follow-ups available for stable, established patients
10. Hospital is smoke-free — no smoking anywhere on the premises

For any queries, call our patient helpline: +91-9876543210
Or email: info@medcarehospital.com
